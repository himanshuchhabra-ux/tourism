## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Q: Can I modify the design?
**A:** Yes! This is a prototype for your review. Please provide feedback and we'll implement changes.

### Q: How do I test on mobile?
**A:** 
- Option 1: Resize browser window
- Option 2: Open index.html on your phone browser
- Option 3: Use Chrome DevTools Device Emulation (F12)

### Q: Why isn't the login working with real credentials?
**A:** This is the design phase - no backend yet. Any email/password will work to demonstrate the flow.

### Q: Can I change the color scheme?
**A:** Absolutely! All colors are editable in styles.css or we can change them for you.

### Q: When will the full application be ready?
**A:** After Phase 1 approval, Phase 2 (User + Vendor Management) will take 2-3 weeks. Total project: 6-7 months across 11 phases.

### Q: What if I need additional features?
**A:** We can add them to the roadmap. Each feature adds time, which we'll re-estimate.

### Q: Can this be deployed now?
**A:** Not recommended for production (no backend). This is for design review and feedback only.
